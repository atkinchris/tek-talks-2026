Security is a trade-off

They didn't get smarter.
The risk changed.

Device 1

Secure element costs money per unit × hundreds of thousands. Against a handful of hobbyists. Not a good trade.

Device 2

Kernel updates and boot chain verification cost development time. Against a few curious people. Not worth it.

Device 3

Some of those curious people turned to piracy. The risk became commercial. Then the investment was justified.
