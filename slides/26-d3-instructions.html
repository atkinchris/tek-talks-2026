How code executes

Instructions are just bytes in memory.

0x1000
MOV R0, #1      <-- PC
0x1004
ADD R1, R0, R1
0x1008
STR R1, [R2]
0x100C
BX LR

Program Counter (PC)

Points to the current instruction. After each instruction, it moves to the next address. Your program is just the PC walking through memory.

Control what the PC points to — control what the processor does.
